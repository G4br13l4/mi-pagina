Pusheen The Cat

Pusheen is a cartoon cat who is the subject of comic strips and sticker sets on Facebook. Pusheen Cat was created in 2010 by Claire Belton and Andrew Duff.

Grumpy Cat

Tardar Sauce, commonly known as Grumpy Cat, is an American cat internet celebrity. She is known for her permanently "grumpy" facial appearance, which is caused by an underbite and feline dwarfism.

Banye, the OMG cat

Meet OMG cat, otherwise known as Banye. This adorable kitty has a dark patch of fur underneath his mouth which makes him look like he is constantly in shock.
